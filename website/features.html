👤 Your Family Profile

One sign-up does it all

The profile is our family questionnaire, made evergreen. Fill it once and it automatically files your family tree entry with the Historian, feeds the yearbook and acknowledgments, tells the Planning Committee what you want from future reunions, and pre-fills every other form on this site with your name, branch, and contact info.

Your dashboard

Once your profile exists, the My Profile page becomes your home base: one-tap dues payments (Zelle, Cash App, or card), pre-filled t-shirt orders, your tree entry, quick links to committees and voting, and a record of everything you've submitted.
