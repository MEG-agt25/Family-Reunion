Committees & Chair Sign-Up 🙋🏾

Eleven standing committees — nine need chairs, and every one needs 2–5 members to assist its chair. Courtesy is led by the Hospitality Chair and Protocol by the Parliamentarian. Pick where you shine and sign up below.

The Standing Committees

🏈 Sports / Games

Tournaments, kids' games, the family Olympics, spades & dominoes brackets.

🎶 Music

DJ & playlists, line-dance sets, the old-school hour, banquet program music.

🍽️ Food

Menus, caterers, the cookout, potluck coordination, the family cookbook.

🦺 Safety

First aid, headcounts, weather plans, venue safety, working with the Sergeant-at-Arms.

🙏 Services

Sunday worship service, prayer, the memorial tribute, elder care & ushers, assigned tables.

📸 Photography

Photographer & videographer booking, branch portraits, yearbook photos, photo scanning.

💝 Philanthropy & Fundraising

Fundraisers for the land-back retainer, scholarships & seed money; scholarship application review.

🗓️ Planning

Dates, venues, decade planning & future locations, budgets, schedules, registration.

💻 Technology

This website, payments & t-shirt store, the family tree tools, group texts & email.

💐 Courtesy led by the Hospitality Chair (Loretta)

Welcome table & check-in, elders' care, ushers & assigned tables, birthdays & accomplishments recognition, get-well & condolence cards (with the Meditation Chair), superlatives ballot count.

📜 Protocol chaired by the Parliamentarian

Keeps meetings running by Robert's Rules: procedure help & training, quorum and attendance count with the Secretary, elections & ballots, keeps the Constitution & Bylaws current.

How Committees Operate

Monthly meetings

Each committee meets monthly (video call counts). Chairs report to the Vice President and give a 3-minute report at every family business meeting.

Money rules

Budget requests go in writing to the Treasurer (Jasmine) before money is spent. The Financial Secretary (Aunt Vanessa) records everything coming in.

Robert's Rules

Committee decisions follow the same simple motion → second → discussion → vote process as the full family meeting. See the Family Business page.

Sub-committees

Chairs may form sub-committees (decade planning, venue scouting, scholarship review) as needed.
